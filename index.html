About Us


Deluto Energy Limited (Deluto) is a privately owned indigenous oil and gas company that was established in 2018. Deluto is focused on developing, providing and operating diverse services ranging from Engineering & Procurement (E&P), supplies and maintenance services to the upstream sector of the oil & gas industry in Nigeria and globally.

Our objective is to provide cutting edge services in Engineering & Procurement (E&P), maintenance and logistics using latest technological innovation.
